What sets Possible Investments apart?


One of our greatest differentiators revolves around regular investment. The concept of dollar-cost averaging which is to invest a regular amount each month (ie regularly) has been around a long time.
However one of its drawbacks is that most product providers simply set this up on the same day of each month, or quarter. With Possible Investments, our algorithms allow you to buy on the biggest dip experienced during the month.

In addition, Possible offer an investment life cycle product. Overtime, if you are committed to regular investment, and your investment grows then your overall holdings will expand accordingly, providing greater diversification which means to invest in a wide array of assets or investments within a portfolio to reduce risk.
