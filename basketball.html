Basketball - a favourite sport of mine since Grade 5




Basketball does have its origins in Canada (where it's Inventor was born), where I am from, and was invented near where Boston University is. Dr James Naismith, pictured here was instrumental in inventing this amazing sport:
In the past 13 years, Stephen Curry has revolutionised the game. At 6'3" he hardly typifies what you would think is valued in basketball: size, but the game has changed so much.
I grew up loving watching Michael Jordan, and my first love was college basketball. I always loved the fact that you had teams made up of individuals that one day would become doctors, financial advisors, teachers, engineers, and they exhibited such team work, especially when they also had massive super stars on their teams.
It's why I love the Warriors since they typify great teamwork, and ball movement. Nowadays, the talking heads in sports that never let it rest will long debate about who is the best this, or best that...i'll leave this here:

As I write this, my favourite team is still the reigning NBA Champions and this is likely to change this week.
